Best 5 Advices For Your Kitchen

At this page you can find 10 best advices for you, which were tested and collected by our team. Let's get started!


COOKHOUSE© 2021

  1. To make the ice transparent, freeze boiled water

    Ice served in restaurants is usually crystal clear and transparent. And the cubes from your refrigerator turn out cloudy and white. But this can be fixed: first boil the water (you can do it twice), only then pour it into the mold.

  2. Always check the age of the eggs to make sure they are fresh

    To do this, lower them into the water:

    • the newly laid egg will lie sideways to the very bottom;
    • a week-old egg will pop up with the blunt end up;
    • an egg at the age of 2-3 weeks will stand in a glassperpendicular to the bottom;
    • a very old egg will float to the surface — it can not be eaten in any case.

  3. Press on the lemon and roll it a little on the surface of the table

    It will be much easier to squeeze out lemon juice this way.

  4. With a spoon, you can peel ginger in a couple of minutes

    Ginger has a rather bizarre shape, so it is not very convenient to clean it with a knife: a lot of excess is cut off. An ordinary tablespoon will cope with this task better.

  5. To peel the garlic, shake it well

    Put the garlic in a bowl and shake it well, holding it with the palm of your hand. Most of the husk will fall off by itself. Instead of a bowl, you can use a jar with a lid.
