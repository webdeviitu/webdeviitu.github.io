ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT FOR INTERNATIONAL COOPERATION

JSC "International University of Information Technologies" pays great attention to international cooperation. The University cooperates with leading foreign universities and IT companies.

PARTNER UNIVERSITIES

In order to successfully compete and attract the best students, MUIT provides a wide range of study abroad programs and attracts foreign teachers and students. Also, the university aims to develop cultural diversity in its curriculum and university environment.

ACADEMIC MOBILITY

Academic mobility allows students to study for one semester at partner universities with credit transfer. This is a unique opportunity for students to get high-quality education in another country and international experience.

DOUBLE DEGREE

Dual degree programs are a unique opportunity to simultaneously get a higher education at the MUIT and at a partner university.

MIXED REALITY LAB

The work of the Mixed Reality Laboratory (MRL) includes the popularization of virtual, augmented and mixed reality technologies, the creation of courses for teaching students, methodological support for laboratory and practical work of students, the introduction of advanced virtual, augmented and mixed reality technologies into the educational process.PUBLICATION ACTIVITY
